Terms and Conditions

Please read our fee and enrollment policies carefully.

1

Advance Monthly Payment

The tuition fee for every month must be paid strictly in advance at the beginning of the respective billing cycle. Students will not be allowed to attend classes if the advance payment is not cleared.

2

General Refund Policy

Under normal circumstances, fees once paid are non-refundable. We encourage students to make full use of our free demo materials before fully committing to the payment.

3

Mid-Month Cancellation Rule

If a student decides to cancel their enrollment exactly halfway through the month (mid-month), a 50% refund of that specific month's fee will be initiated.

4

Zero Extra Fees for 10th Boards

The special 10th Boards preparation plan (including PYQs, pre-boards, and question banks) is entirely covered under the standard fee structure. There are no extra or hidden charges for this special plan.
